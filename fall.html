Beef Stew VI

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cubed beef stew meat
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cubes beef bouillon, crumbled
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 large potatoes peeled and cubed
  • 4 carrots cut in 1 inch pieces
  • 4 stalks of celery cut in 1 inch pieces
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp cold water

Recipe

  1. In a large pot,cook beef in oil over medium heat until brown.
  2. Dissolve bouillon in water and pour into pot.
  3. Stir in rosemary, parsley, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 1 hour.
  5. Stir potatoes, carrots, celery, and onion into the pot.
  6. Dissolve cornstarch in 2 teaspoons cold water and stir into stew.
  7. Cover and simmer 1 hour more.
